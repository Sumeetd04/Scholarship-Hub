Application Timeline

Student

Submit Application

Submit with documents at college within 15 days of admission. Complete Aadhaar biometric authentication at Meeseva.

Student

OTR Registration

Complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the NSP using Aadhaar or EID. Valid for your entire academic career.

College / Principal

Verification & Forwarding

College verifies and forwards for biometric authentication, submits hardcopy to district office.

District Officer

Sanction & Disbursal

District welfare officer sanctions and releases funds. Utilization certificates due within 15 days of RTF credit.
